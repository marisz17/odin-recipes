Skopo

Description

Skopo, otherwise known as “Smiley” is a classic South African delicacy. The meal consisting of a sheep head gained notoriety amongst Zulu taxi drivers and is now found at every taxi rank or township corner throughout South Africa.

Ingredients

  • Sheep head
  • 2 onions
  • 2 chilli beef cubes
  • Boiling water
  • Brown onion soup
  • Olive Oil
  • Barbecue spice
  • Steak & Chops spice

Steps

  1. If you want to go the traditional route, we suggest you use 2 chilli beef stock cubes so that the Skopo has a flavour-filled base to marinate in.
  2. Chop the onions into smaller pieces
  3. Place stove on medium heat
  4. Add boiling water to large pot
  5. Add the Skopo to the large pot of boiling water
  6. Add the chopped onion pieces
  7. Boil for 30 minutes before adding the chilli beef stock
  8. Boil for another hour or so and add brown onion soup to create a sauce for your skopo
  9. (Always have a kettle of boiling water ready incase your pot gets dry)
  10. Continue cooking the meal while stirring for an hour and a half or 2 hours (Depends on how soft your want the meat to be)
  11. Once satisfied with the texture of your meat you can switch off the stove and let it simmer
  12. One can then either decide to eat as is with pap but because it’s festive and every meal is a statement, we going gourmet and making further changes.
  13. Remove your Skopo from the chilli beef broth and place in an oven pan.
  14. Add your favourite spices but we suggest barbecue spice and steak and chops.
  15. Add a little olive oil and place in the oven for 15 minutes.
  16. When the Skopo is golden brown and the smell can’t be ignored, then you know it’s ready.
